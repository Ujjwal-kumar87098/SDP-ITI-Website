I.T.I - Electrician

Intermediate

2 years trade in ITI will be eligible for qualification equivalent to ITI. Candidate will have to give only language paper Exam (Hindi + English) for Intermediate degree.

Intermediate + Electrician [2 Years]

ITI Electrician is a 2-year regular course offered by Industrial Training Institute under the Craftsman Training Scheme (CTS). ITI Electrician course trains the students to become an electrician and work at the industrial level.

Fitter & Diesel Mechanics

ITI Diesel Mechanic is a programme that teaches the students about various aspects of diesel engines such as making alignments & adjustments, correcting deficiencies in vehicles, maintaining pieces of equipment operating in diesel, etc.

Our facilities

The institute provides good study environment, well educated and trained engineering Drawing Instructor, Qualified National Craft Instructor Exam conducted by NCVT and all staff and administration have good educational background (preferably B.Tech/ M.A/ITI/Diploma/ 3 Years+ teaching experience)

Campus Placement by NCVT And Various Motor Companies

Every year NCVT directly recruits ITI Trainees for various Power Plant. They start their recruitment drive, Also Bihar Rozgar Mela is organized every 3-4 months in different districts of government ITIs to enhance the skills of ITI trainees and do internship in various government organizations.

Scholarship Availability

The institute is registered with Post Matric Scholarship, Bihar, so every year the Government of Bihar gives scholarship to complete higher studies, which helps each trainee financially to do the course. Rs 5000-6500 is given for the scholarship amount by the Government of Bihar.
